About Me

Bio

My name is Drew Erny. I'm a software developer living in San Francisco.

I currently work at Docker on Swarmkit, the container orchestration engine powering Docker's Swarm Mode. I also work on some of Docker's internal test platform automation tooling, and I designed an internal end-to-end framework for testing Docker's commerical and open source projects.

My interests are primarily in distributed systems and back end services.

At home, I enjoy political activism, making things, and amateur radio.
